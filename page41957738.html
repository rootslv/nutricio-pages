How to delete Your Account?

Instructions:

We understand that you may wish to delete your account. This action will permanently remove your account and all associated data from our system. Please note that this action is irreversible.

Steps to follow:

Confirm your request: Fill the form below to confirm your request to delete your account.
Enter your Account ID: Please enter your Account ID in the field below. This is a security measure to ensure that you are the owner of the account.
Provide your email: Please enter the email address associated with your account. We will send a confirmation email to this address to ensure the request is legitimate.
Reason for deletion (optional): If you wish, you can provide a reason for your decision to delete your account. This will help us understand your needs better and improve our services.
Submit your request: Click on the 'Submit ' button to send your request

Note:

  • We will retain your account information for a period of 30 days from the date of your request. After this period, your account will be permanently deleted.
  • We will remove all your personal data from our system, including your Account ID and email address.
  • If you wish to reactivate your account in the future, you will need to create a new account.

Contact Us:
If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team at rootslava@gmail.com.
